Khayru-l khalqi ʿindallāh, ṣāḥibu-l ʿizzi wa-l jāh, khātamu rusuli-l Ilāh, Muḥammadur Rasūlullāh

The best of creation in the sight of Allah, the possessor of might and station, the seal of the Messengers of God, is Muhammad ﷺ, the Messenger of Allah
